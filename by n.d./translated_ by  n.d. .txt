


Chapter One: Enrollment Examination
Aili was a girl from a small village, yet she possessed formidable magical talent.

During the enrollment examination at the Magic Academy, she demonstrated astonishing abilities.
"The girl's magical power is beyond imagination," the Dean exclaimed in astonishment.

Chapter Two: New Companions
In the academy, Aili made many new friends.
There was Tom, adept at fire magic, and Lily, an expert in healing arts.
"Let us strive together to become the strongest magicians," Aili said to her companions.

Chapter Three: The First Mission
The academy assigned new students their first practical mission.
They were to venture deep into the forest and eliminate the beasts there.
"This will be our chance to prove ourselves," Aili tightened her grip on her wand, her eyes filled with determination.
